## What this is

A complete, working appointment-booking website for your barbershop — not a template. It already includes a live database, customer accounts, automated confirmation emails, a full admin dashboard, and built-in security.

### Customer side
- Browse services, pick barber, date & time
- Sign in by email (one-time code — no passwords to forget)
- Get a booking reference + **automatic confirmation email**
- View, reschedule, or cancel their own appointments
- Leave reviews after their visit

### Admin / shop side
- Secure staff login
- Dashboard, calendar (month & day view), records, customer list
- Create and manage bookings
- Moderate customer reviews
- Auto "no-show" marking for late customers

### Under the hood (the part that makes it real software)
- Supabase (PostgreSQL) cloud database — real data, not fake
- Row-level security so customer data stays private
- Login throttling + password reset
- Email delivery via Resend
